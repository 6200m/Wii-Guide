This tutorial will explain you how to install Wii Mod Lite and IOS on your Wii.

![RiiConnect24 Logo](/images/WiiRC24Logo.jpg)

#### What you need
* And SD Card with at least 128 MB of free space.
* Internet access on your computer.

#### Instructions

##### Section I - Downloading

1. [Download the latest version of Wii Mod Lite from GitHub (the .zip archive).(https://github.com/RiiConnect24/Wii-Mod-Lite/releases)
1. Open the .zip file that you downloaded and copy the content of it to SD Card.
![SD Card Drag and Drop](/images/WiiModLite/01.gif)
1. After it will be done. Copy the patched IOS files to and folder `WAD` on an SD Card.

If you want to see how to use IOS Patcher, click here!
{: .notice--info}

1. After that IOSs will be on SD Card, put it back to Wii.

##### Section II - Installing
1. Run the Homebrew Channel on your Wii.

![Homebrew Channel Select](/images/WiiModLite/02.jpg)
![Homebrew Channel Launch](/images/WiiModLite/03.jpg)

1. Launch Wii Mod Lite.

![Homebrew Channel List](/images/WiiModLite/04.jpg)
![Homebrew Channel Load](/images/WiiModLite/05.jpg)

1. Once Wii Mod Lite will be running, choose `WAD Manager`.

![Wii Mod Lite WAD MANAGER](/images/WiiModLite/06.jpg)

1. Choose `Wii SD Slot`.

![Wii SD Slot](/images/WiiModLite/07.jpg)

1. You should now see 2 files. Select them and press + on your Wii Remote.

![Select them](/images/WiiModLite/howtoselect.gif)

1. After you will select them, press "A" twice and start installing.

![Install](/images/WiiModLite/08.jpg)
![Install](/images/WiiModLite/09.jpg)

After it will finish installing, that's it!

![Complete](/images/WiiModLite/10.jpg)
