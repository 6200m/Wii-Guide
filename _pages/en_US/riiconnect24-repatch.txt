For security reasons, existing RiiConnect24 users need to repatch their nwc24msg.cfg in order to use Wii Mail again. It is easier than before to patch it, and it shouldn't take long.

[If you never have installed RiiConnect24, please follow the guide here.](riiconnect24)<br>
{: .notice--info}

#### What you need
* An SD Card
* [RiiConnect24 Mail Patcher](https://github.com/RiiConnect24/Mail-Patcher/releases)

#### Instructions

##### Section I - Downloading

1. Download RiiConnect24 Mail Patcher from the link above, and extract it to your SD card.
1. Put your SD Card in your Wii.

##### Section II - Installing

1. Launch the Homebrew Channel on your Wii.
1. Launch RiiConnect24 Mail Patcher.
1. It should only take a few seconds to patch your nwc24msg.cfg. When it's done, press HOME to exit.
